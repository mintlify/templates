title: "Benefits overview"
description: "Health, dental, vision, 401(k), and other perks available to full-time Mintlify employees."
---

Benefits are available to all full-time employees starting on your first day. Part-time and contract workers should check with the People team for what applies.

## Health insurance

We offer medical coverage through our benefits provider. Mintlify covers a substantial portion of the premium for employees and their dependents. Plan options and current premium splits are in the HR portal — enroll within 30 days of your start date, or during the annual open enrollment window in November.

## Dental and vision

- **Dental** — Employee coverage included. Covers two cleanings and X-rays per year at 100%.
- **Vision** — Employee coverage included. Annual eye exam covered, plus an allowance for glasses or contacts.

## 401(k)

We offer a 401(k) with company match. Enroll through the HR portal — changes take effect the following pay period. Ask People for current match details.

## Equity

All full-time employees receive an equity grant. See [equity overview](/hr-people/compensation/equity-overview) for how vesting works and what to do at key milestones.

## Other perks

- **Home office stipend** — One-time stipend for new hires to set up a productive workspace. See [home office setup](/policies/remote-work/home-office-setup).
- **Learning & development** — Annual budget for courses, books, or conferences. Submit through the HR portal.
- **Wellness** — Monthly reimbursement for gym, fitness apps, or mental health services.
- **Parental leave** — Generous paid leave for primary and secondary caregivers. Contact People for current policy details.

## Enrollment and changes

Log in to the HR portal to enroll, view plan details, or make changes during a qualifying life event (marriage, birth, etc.).

Questions? Reach the People team in `#people-help` on Slack.
