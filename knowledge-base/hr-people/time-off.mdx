Mintlify has an unlimited PTO policy for salaried employees. Unlimited means we trust you to take what you need — it doesn't mean time off is unlimited in practice. Most people take three to five weeks per year, plus company holidays.

## Types of leave

**Vacation / personal time** — No fixed accrual. Take time off by following the request process below. We encourage you to actually use it.

**Sick leave** — Use sick days when you're ill or need to care for a sick family member. No approval needed — notify your manager and mark it in the HR portal.

**Company holidays** — We observe approximately 10 federal holidays per year. The full calendar is posted in Notion each January.

**Parental leave** — Generous paid leave for primary and secondary caregivers. Contact People for current policy.

**Bereavement** — Up to 5 days paid leave, no approval needed. Contact People for longer situations.

**Jury duty / civic leave** — Fully paid. Send documentation to People.

## Requesting time off

1. Check with your manager first — especially around launches, on-call rotations, or team offsites.
2. Submit your request in the HR portal at least **two weeks in advance** for absences longer than two days.
3. Block your Google Calendar and set an out-of-office reply.
4. For sick days, submit in the portal on the day or the next business day.

## Blackout periods

Some periods are high-stakes for Mintlify — major launches, fundraising, or key customer commitments. Your manager will communicate these in advance. Requests during a blackout period require manager approval.

## Carryover

Because PTO is unlimited, there is nothing to carry over or pay out at termination.
