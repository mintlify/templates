Mintlify uses salary bands tied to levels to make compensation decisions consistent and defensible. Bands are reviewed annually against market data from sources like Levels.fyi and Radford.

## Leveling

Each role has a level, typically L1 through L5 for individual contributors (or equivalent titles: Associate, Mid, Senior, Staff, Principal). Levels reflect scope of impact, autonomy, and expected contributions — not just years of experience.

Your offer letter includes your level. If you're unsure of your level, ask your manager.

## Bands

Bands have a minimum, midpoint, and maximum. We target offers at or near the midpoint for candidates who are a strong fit for the role and level.

| Level | Example title | Notes |
|-------|---------------|-------|
| L1 | Associate Engineer / Associate PM | Early career, close guidance |
| L2 | Engineer / PM | Independent execution on defined scope |
| L3 | Senior Engineer / Senior PM | Independent, drives projects end to end |
| L4 | Staff Engineer / Group PM | Cross-team impact, raises the bar |
| L5 | Principal Engineer | Company-wide technical strategy |

Specific band ranges are available in the HR portal. Ask People if you need access.

## Pay adjustments

Compensation adjustments happen through:

1. **Annual review cycle** — Merit increases tied to performance ratings.
2. **Promotions** — Moving to a higher level typically brings pay to the midpoint of the new band.
3. **Market corrections** — If band data shows a role is below market, we may adjust outside the annual cycle.

## Geographic adjustments

Mintlify is a remote-first company. We use location-adjusted bands based on cost of labor in your region. Ask People for your specific band range.
