Equity is a meaningful part of total compensation at Mintlify. This page explains the basics — for personalized details, review your grant agreement or contact People.

## Grant type

Mintlify currently issues **stock options (ISOs for US employees)** — the right to purchase shares at the strike price set at your grant date. Options have value if Mintlify's fair market value rises above your strike price.

Your offer letter specifies the number of shares and your strike price.

## Vesting schedule

Standard vesting is **4 years with a 1-year cliff**:

- No shares vest until your 1-year anniversary at Mintlify
- 25% of your grant vests on the cliff date
- The remaining 75% vests monthly over the following 36 months

Refresher grants for tenured employees vest on the same monthly schedule without a cliff.

## Exercising options

Options must be exercised to convert to shares. You have a window to exercise:

- **While employed** — any time after vesting
- **After leaving** — typically 90 days from your last day (check your specific grant agreement)

Tax implications vary — ISOs have favorable tax treatment but require careful timing. Talk to a tax advisor before exercising, especially if the amount is significant.

## Equity events

At a liquidity event (IPO or acquisition), vested shares become tradeable or are converted to cash. Unvested shares may be subject to acceleration clauses in acquisition scenarios — check your grant agreement.
