title: "Access requests"
description: "How to request access to Mintlify tools, systems, and data — and what to expect."
---

All access to Mintlify's systems follows the principle of least privilege: you get access to what you need to do your job, not more.

## How to request access

1. Post in `#general` on Slack with: the tool or system you need, the level of access, and your business justification.
2. Your manager will confirm the request in the thread.
3. Most standard tools are fulfilled within **one business day**. Production or customer data systems require additional review and may take up to three business days.

## Access levels

| Level | Examples | Approver |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Standard | Slack channels, Notion, Figma, Linear | Manager |
| Elevated | Vercel, staging environments, PostHog | Manager |
| Privileged | AWS production, customer data | Manager + founders |
| Admin | Identity provider, Stripe billing | Founders only |

## Shared credentials and service accounts

Do not share personal credentials. If a workflow requires a service account or shared secret, request one through `#general`. Shared credentials are audited quarterly.

## Access reviews

We review access periodically. You may receive a Slack message listing your current access — review it and flag anything you no longer need.

## Offboarding

When a team member leaves, all access is revoked on their last day. Managers should notify People at least two days in advance so handoffs can be arranged.

## Urgent access issues

For urgent access issues blocking critical work, post in `#general` and include your manager. Someone will respond quickly.
