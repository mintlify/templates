---
title: "Device policy"
description: "What's required on Mintlify devices and what you can and can't do with them."
---

All devices used to access Mintlify systems — whether company-issued or personal — must meet our minimum security requirements.

## Company-issued devices

Mintlify issues MacBook laptops to all full-time employees. Company laptops are enrolled in MDM (Kolide + Apple MDM) automatically and enforce:

- Full-disk encryption (FileVault)
- Screen lock after inactivity
- Approved macOS versions
- CrowdStrike endpoint security agent
- Remote wipe capability

Do not attempt to remove MDM profiles or disable security software. Doing so will revoke your access to Mintlify systems and may result in disciplinary action.

## Personal devices (BYOD)

If you use a personal device for Slack or email, you must:

1. Enable a screen lock (PIN, password, or biometric)
2. Enable full-disk encryption
3. Keep the OS up to date
4. Not store sensitive Mintlify or customer data locally

Personal devices are not enrolled in MDM. If IT needs to take action on a personal device in a security incident, only managed work apps will be affected.

## Approved and prohibited uses

**Approved:**
- Work tasks, communication, and collaboration
- Personal use that doesn't interfere with security or performance

**Prohibited:**
- Installing software that circumvents security controls (e.g., disabling CrowdStrike)
- Storing customer data on personal cloud storage — use Notion, Google Drive, or approved systems
- Connecting company laptops to untrusted public Wi-Fi without Tailscale connected
- Sharing your device with others for work tasks

## Lost or stolen devices

Report lost or stolen devices to the People team **immediately** via Slack or email. We will remotely lock the device and initiate a wipe if necessary.

## End of employment

Return company devices on or before your last day. Retained devices are subject to remote wipe and recovery.
