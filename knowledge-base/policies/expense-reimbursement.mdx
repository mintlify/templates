Mintlify reimburses reasonable, work-related expenses. "Reasonable" means what a thoughtful person would spend of their own money on a business task.

## What's covered

| Category | Covered | Not covered |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| Travel (business trips) | Flights, trains, hotels within policy | First-class upgrades (unless pre-approved), personal side trips |
| Meals (travel days) | Up to $75/day | Alcohol (unless a team event pre-approved by a founder) |
| Home office | See [home office setup](/policies/remote-work/home-office-setup) | Furniture beyond the one-time stipend |
| Software and tools | Role-specific tools with manager approval | Subscriptions that duplicate Mintlify-provided tools |
| Team events | Team lunches and offsites with manager approval | Events without prior budget approval |
| Learning & development | Courses, books, conferences within annual budget | Items outside the L&D budget |

When in doubt, ask your manager before spending.

## How to submit

1. Log in to the expense tool (link in the HR portal).
2. Create a new report and attach receipts for every line item over $25.
3. Select the appropriate expense category and add a brief business justification.
4. Submit for manager approval.

**Deadline:** Submit expenses within **30 days** of the expense date. Late submissions may be declined.

## Approval and reimbursement

- Manager approves the report.
- Finance reviews within 3 business days.
- Reimbursement is paid via direct deposit on the next payroll cycle.

## Corporate card

Some roles have a corporate card for recurring or high-volume expenses. Corporate card expenses still require receipts and business justifications in the expense tool.

## Travel bookings

Use the approved travel booking tool for flights and hotels. Booking outside the tool requires prior approval.
