We want every team member to have a workspace that lets them do their best work. Here's what's available and how to use it.

## New hire stipend

Full-time employees receive a **one-time home office stipend** to spend on equipment and furniture. Submit receipts through the expense tool within 90 days of your start date. See [expense reimbursement](/policies/expense-reimbursement) for the submission process.

Contact People for the current stipend amount.

### What's covered

- Monitor, keyboard, mouse, or other peripherals
- Desk, chair, or ergonomic furniture
- Lighting or webcam for video calls
- Headset or headphones

### What's not covered

- General household items that aren't primarily for work
- Upgrades to items already provided by Mintlify

## Company-provided equipment

IT ships your MacBook before your start date. If your role requires additional equipment (external monitor, docking station), request it in `#general` — your manager will need to confirm.

## Internet reimbursement

We reimburse a monthly amount for home internet. Submit monthly through the expense tool using the "Internet" category. No pre-approval needed. Contact People for the current reimbursement cap.

## Co-working spaces

If you prefer not to work from home, we'll reimburse a monthly co-working space membership with manager approval. Submit through the expense tool. Contact People for the current monthly cap.

## Ergonomics

If you're experiencing discomfort from your setup, contact People. We can arrange an ergonomic assessment and cover additional equipment.
