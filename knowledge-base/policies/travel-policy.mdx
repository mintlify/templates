Business travel should be booked through the approved travel tool. This ensures centralized billing, spend visibility, and compliance with our limits.

## Before you book

Get your manager's approval before booking any trip. For trips with a total estimated cost over $1,000, a founder should also be in the loop.

Once approved, book through the travel tool using your Mintlify email.

## Booking guidelines

**Flights**
- Book economy class for flights under 5 hours.
- Business class is permitted for flights over 8 hours. Get manager approval before booking.
- Book at least 14 days in advance when possible to reduce cost.

**Hotels**
- Rate cap: $200/night in most cities; $300/night in high-cost cities (San Francisco, New York, London, etc.).
- Exceptions require manager approval before booking.

**Ground transport**
- Rideshare or public transit for airport transfers.
- Rental cars are approved for multi-stop trips or locations without adequate transit.

## On the road

Keep receipts for everything. Submit expenses within 30 days of returning — see [expense reimbursement](/policies/expense-reimbursement) for the full process.

Meals are covered at up to $75/day for each travel day. Team dinners can exceed this with prior manager approval.

## Safety

Register your trip in the travel tool so the company knows your itinerary. If you're traveling to a country with a travel advisory, contact People before booking.

In an emergency abroad: contact your travel insurer (details in the travel tool), then notify your manager.

## Cancellations

If a trip needs to be cancelled, do so as early as possible through the travel tool to minimize cancellation fees. Non-refundable bookings require manager approval before booking.
