All company laptops are Macs shipped with macOS and endpoint management pre-installed. Do not wipe or reimage your machine without IT approval.

## First boot

1. Power on and follow the macOS setup wizard. Sign in with your `@mintlify.com` Google account when prompted.
2. MDM (Mobile Device Management) will install automatically within a few minutes of connecting to Wi-Fi. Do not dismiss any MDM prompts.
3. You'll receive a Slack message from IT confirming enrollment. If it doesn't arrive within 30 minutes, post in `#general`.

## Required software (auto-installed via MDM)

IT pushes the following automatically — you don't need to install these manually:

- **1Password** — password manager
- **CrowdStrike** — endpoint security agent
- **Kolide** — device health monitoring
- **Tailscale** — VPN (see [VPN setup](/onboarding/tools/vpn-setup))

If any are missing after 24 hours, post in `#general`.

## Role-specific setup

For engineers, follow the [developer setup guide](/engineering/dev-setup) after completing the steps above. Your tech lead will walk you through anything team-specific on your first day.

## FileVault (disk encryption)

Full-disk encryption is enforced by MDM. Verify it's active:

**System Settings → Privacy & Security → FileVault**

It should show "FileVault is turned on." If not, MDM will enable it automatically — you may be prompted to restart.

## Issues

For hardware problems or missing software, post in `#general` on Slack. Do not attempt to fix hardware issues yourself — it may void the warranty.
