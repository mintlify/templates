Mintlify uses Tailscale for VPN. The client is installed automatically by MDM — this guide covers connecting for the first time and resolving common issues.

## First connection

1. Open **Tailscale** from your Applications folder or menu bar.
2. Click **Log in** and authenticate with your `@mintlify.com` Google account.
3. You'll see a "Connected" status in the menu bar icon within a few seconds.
4. Your device is now on the Mintlify network.

## When VPN is required

Tailscale is required to access:

- Internal staging and development environments
- Database admin tools
- Internal dashboards (monitoring, logs)

VPN is **not** required for: email, Slack, Linear, Notion, GitHub, Figma, or Vercel — these use SSO directly.

## Split tunnel

Tailscale is configured as split tunnel by default — only traffic destined for Mintlify's internal network routes through the VPN. Normal internet traffic goes direct. Do not change this configuration.

## Staying connected

Tailscale reconnects automatically after sleep or network changes. If it disconnects and doesn't reconnect, click the menu bar icon and select **Connect**.

## Troubleshooting

**Not showing as connected after login** — Make sure MDM enrollment completed successfully. See [laptop setup](/onboarding/tools/laptop-setup).

**Connected but can't reach an internal service** — Try disconnecting and reconnecting Tailscale. If the issue persists, post in `#general` with the URL you're trying to reach.

**Kicked out of Tailscale network** — Your device may have failed a health check (e.g., OS out of date, FileVault disabled). Check Kolide in your menu bar for any flagged issues.

Still stuck? Post in `#general` on Slack.
