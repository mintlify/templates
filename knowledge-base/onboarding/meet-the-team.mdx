## Teams

### Engineering

Builds and maintains the Mintlify platform: the docs rendering engine, deployment pipeline, web editor, analytics, and API integrations. Home base is `#eng` on Slack.

### Product

Owns the product roadmap, user research, and feature prioritization. Works closely with engineering and design on new capabilities. Home base is `#product`.

### Design

Responsible for the Mintlify product UI, brand, and docs site component library. Partners with engineering on all customer-facing features. Home base is `#design`.

### Go-to-Market

Covers sales, marketing, and customer success. Works to grow Mintlify's customer base and ensure customers succeed with the product. Home base is `#gtm`.

### People & Operations

Handles recruiting, HR, onboarding, and company operations. Your first stop for benefits, payroll, or policy questions. Reach them at `#people-help`.

## Finding anyone

- **Slack directory** — Search by name or role in the Members section of Slack.
- **Google Directory** — Search [contacts.google.com](https://contacts.google.com) with your Mintlify account.
- **Linear** — Search for teammates by name to see what they're working on.

If you're not sure who owns something, ask in `#general` on Slack — someone will route you to the right person.
