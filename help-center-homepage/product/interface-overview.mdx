## Layout

- **Sidebar** — Projects, automation, and account entry points.
- **Canvas** — The main surface for viewing and editing work.
- **Inspector** — Metadata and contextual actions for the current selection.

## Command palette

Open the palette to jump anywhere quickly. It respects your permissions.

## Settings

Workspace settings live under your avatar; project settings are scoped per project.
