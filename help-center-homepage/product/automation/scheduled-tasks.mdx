## Schedules

Use cron expressions or friendly presets. All times respect the project timezone unless overridden.

## Daylight saving

Ambiguous local times may skip or repeat—prefer UTC for critical jobs.

## Monitoring

Failed runs surface in the automation console with logs for debugging.
