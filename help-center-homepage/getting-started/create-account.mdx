## Sign up

Visit the Banyan signup page and enter your work email. We send a one-time link to verify ownership of the address.

## Organization type

- **Company** — Multiple teams under one billing profile.
- **Personal** — Individual use with the option to upgrade later.

## Verification issues

If the email does not arrive within a few minutes, check spam folders and ensure your IT allowlist includes Banyan notification domains.
