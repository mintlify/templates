## Prerequisites

Admin access in Salesforce and a Banyan admin to approve the connector.

## Field mapping

Map accounts, contacts, and opportunities to Banyan entities. Resolve duplicates by external ID where possible.

## Sync modes

Choose one-way or two-way sync depending on which system owns each field.
