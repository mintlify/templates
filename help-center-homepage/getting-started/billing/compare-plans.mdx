## Highlights

- **Free** — Core projects and limited automation runs.
- **Team** — SSO options, higher limits, and priority email support.
- **Enterprise** — Regional options, advanced audit, and dedicated support channels.

## How to choose

Start where your current limits hurt first—usually automation volume or member count—then add Enterprise when procurement or compliance requires it.
