## Where to find invoices

Navigate to **Settings → Billing → Invoices** for PDF copies and line items.

## Tax and VAT

Update tax IDs before renewal to avoid delays. This demo does not constitute tax advice.

## Receipts

Card charges include a receipt link suitable for expense tools that accept emailed PDFs.
