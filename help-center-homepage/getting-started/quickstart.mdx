## Before you begin

- A verified email you can access
- Admin access if you are enabling SSO later

## Steps

1. **Create your organization** — Pick a name your team will recognize in notifications.
2. **Invite teammates** — Start with a small pilot group before rolling out widely.
3. **Connect one integration** — Choose Slack or your CRM to see data flow end to end.
4. **Create a first project** — Use it as a sandbox for workflows and permissions.
